Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I make a booking?

To make a booking, you can click on the "Book now" button on the home screen and it will redirect you to a booking form. Just fill out the form and you're done!

Can I print the Booking Receipt?

Yes. After you successfully made a booking, you will be given an option to print the receipt

How can I pay for my booking?

You are able to pay for your booking when you have arrived here at our sports centre!

Is there a time limit for my booking?

Yes, you are only able to book for only 3 hours maximum. If you would like to continue your time, please make another booking at our registration counter

How can I receive updates about the sport centre?

You can view our social media to view much more about our sport centre and receive real-time update
